### Inline Math

This is an inline math $x^2 + y^2 = z^2$

### Block Math

This is a block math:

$$
\begin{align}
x^2 + y^2 &= z^2 \\
x^2 &= z^2 - y^2 \\
x &= \sqrt{z^2 - y^2}
\end{align}
$$
